# Hose Local Market Order Sheet - 2026-05-04

Purpose: one sheet to send to a local hose/rubber/hydraulic market for the Toyota Land Cruiser J40/HJ47 with Toyota 2H diesel. The lengths below are the buy lengths. Final trimming is done on the vehicle unless the item is a molded hose or a complete hydraulic assembly.

Detailed engineering controls stay in [engine-hose-tube-replacement-specs.md](engine-hose-tube-replacement-specs.md) and [replacement-pipes-workstream.md](replacement-pipes-workstream.md).

## Message To Shop

Please supply or quote the exact items below for a Toyota Land Cruiser J40/HJ47 with 2H diesel. Use automotive-rated hose only. Send photos of hose printing, packaging, end fittings, clamp style, and price before payment.

Brake and clutch flex hoses must be complete crimped hydraulic assemblies copied from the old assembly or matched to the current vehicle fittings. Do not supply generic rubber hose for brake or clutch hydraulics.

## Hard Rules

- No clear PVC, washer hose, or unmarked soft hose for fuel.
- No generic rubber hose for brake or clutch hydraulics.
- No sharp perforated worm clamps on diesel hoses.
- No fuel hose as a substitute for radiator or heater hose.
- No coolant-only hose for oil breather or oil-mist routes.
- No bare copper for brake or fuel hard lines.
- Do not coat the formed coolant pipe until it passes dry-fit and pressure test.
