# Engine Hose And Tube Replacement Specs

Vehicle basis: the active project baseline is the fitted Toyota 2H diesel in a J40/HJ47-style installation. Because this is not a simple stock 1978 petrol engine bay, molded hose purchases still need a final match against the actual radiator, thermostat housing, heater core, fuel filter, injection pump, and vacuum pump fittings before payment.

## Material Rules

- Coolant and heater hoses: EPDM coolant hose, SAE J20R3 for heater/small coolant and SAE J20R4 or molded equivalent for radiator hose. Do not use fuel hose for main coolant runs.
- Fuel hoses: diesel-rated SAE J30R9, SAE J30R14T2, or DIN 73379-3E. Do not use clear PVC, washer hose, vacuum hose, or generic coolant hose for diesel.
- Oil/vapour/breather hoses: oil-resistant NBR or fuel/oil-rated rubber. EPDM coolant hose swells in oil service.
- Vacuum lines: reinforced vacuum hose for brake booster/vacuum pump circuits. Thin soft hose can collapse and remove brake assist.
- Routing: every hose should have a gentle radius, no rubbing on fan/belts/exhaust, no unsupported long spans, and new clamps after final routing.

## Local Buying Script

Take the old hoses or caliper measurements and ask for:

1. `16571-68020` upper radiator hose and `16572-68020` lower radiator hose for Toyota 2H HJ47.
2. EPDM heater hose pieces matching Toyota `99552-30500 L=400` and `99552-30300 L=280`, or 16 mm / 5/8 inch EPDM heater hose cut to measured length.
3. Diesel fuel hose: 8 mm ID x 3 m, 6 mm ID x 2 m, 3.2 or 3.5 mm leak-off x 1 m, all SAE J30R9/J30R14T2 or DIN 73379-3E.
4. Reinforced vacuum hose matched to the brake booster/vacuum pump barbs, plus the correct one-way check valve if the existing valve is old or unknown.
5. Smooth-band coolant clamps, fuel-injection clamps, and rubber-lined P-clips for hard-line support.
