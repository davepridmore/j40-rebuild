# Engine Hose And Tube Replacement Specs

Vehicle basis: the active project baseline is the fitted Toyota 2H diesel in a J40/HJ47-style installation. Because this is not a simple stock 1978 petrol engine bay, molded hose purchases still need a final match against the actual radiator, thermostat housing, heater core, fuel filter, injection pump, and vacuum pump fittings before payment.

Supplier-facing handoff: use [Longman Pipe And Hose Order Spec](longman-pipe-hose-order-spec-20260512.md) when sending the job to Longman Mills. This page keeps the detailed spec and source notes.

## Material Rules

- Coolant and heater hoses: EPDM coolant hose, SAE J20R3 for heater/small coolant and SAE J20R4 or molded equivalent for radiator hose. Do not use fuel hose for main coolant runs.
- Fuel hoses: diesel-rated SAE J30R9, SAE J30R14T2, or DIN 73379-3E. Do not use clear PVC, washer hose, vacuum hose, or generic coolant hose for diesel.
- Oil/vapour/breather hoses: oil-resistant NBR or fuel/oil-rated rubber. EPDM coolant hose swells in oil service.
- Vacuum lines: reinforced vacuum hose for brake booster/vacuum pump circuits. Thin soft hose can collapse and remove brake assist.
- Engine air-intake ducting: use intake/air-duct rubber that tolerates oil mist and engine movement. Do not substitute heater, fuel, washer, or thin HVAC duct hose.
- Routing: every hose should have a gentle radius, no rubbing on fan/belts/exhaust, no unsupported long spans, and new clamps after final routing.

## Longman Quote Script

Send the old hoses or caliper measurements and ask Longman for new replacement parts:

1. Molded EPDM upper and lower radiator hoses for Toyota 2H HJ47 route; use `16571-68020`, `16572-68020`, Dayco `DMH1342` / `CH1342` upper, and Dayco `DMH1343` / `CH1343` lower as shape references. Vendor-facing free-length basis: upper `355 mm`, lower `480 mm`. Confirm neck ODs, clamp OD, and old-hose free length in `mm`.
2. Formed metal coolant/radiator pipe: `28-30 mm OD`, `1.2-1.6 mm` wall tube; buy/quote `1000 mm` shop stock if sold by meter, with `750 mm` as the absolute minimum blank; trim only after the physical sample's centerline length is measured.
3. Two formed-pipe connector hoses: ask for `2 x 500 mm` EPDM radiator rubber pipe blanks, working `28-30 mm ID`, then cut by measured pipe-end OD, mating spigot OD, finished cut length, hose overlap, bend/kink clearance, and clamp OD in `mm`; use molded connectors if straight hose kinks.
4. EPDM heater hose: inlet `400 mm` cut length and outlet `280 mm` cut length, `16 mm` / `5/8 inch` ID exact order basis.
5. Diesel fuel hose: `8 mm ID x 1500 mm` feed hose for the measured approx `1200 mm` tank-to-engine route, `6 mm ID x 2000 mm` return/bleed hose, `3.2-3.5 mm` leak-off x `1000 mm`, all SAE J30R9/J30R14T2 or DIN 73379-3E.
6. Reinforced vacuum hose `10-12 mm ID x 2000 mm` and oil-resistant breather hose `16-19 mm ID x 1000 mm`, cut to measured route, plus the correct one-way check valve if the existing valve is old or unknown.
7. New engine air-cleaner intake duct/couplers: sample-match by air-cleaner outlet OD, intake inlet OD, free length, offset, accordion travel, and clamp OD in `mm`.
8. Smooth-band coolant clamps, fuel-injection clamps, and rubber-lined P-clips matched to measured hose/tube OD in `mm`.
