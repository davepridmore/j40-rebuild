# Rubber Recreation Photo Selection

Purpose: selected collection driving local recreation of the body-mount/front-support rubber parts, long strip/channel pieces, sleeves, shims, and sample stacks.

Source imports: Google Photos picker runs `20260502T013759` and `20260517T213118`.

Selection tracker: `data/manual/rubber_recreation_photo_selection.csv`.

Fabrication handoff: [rubber-recreation-fabrication-spec-20260502.md](rubber-recreation-fabrication-spec-20260502.md).

## Selection Rules

- Keep photos that clearly show rubber shape, thickness, center bore, sleeve/crush tube, washer seat, shim stack, or station/location.
- Prefer images with a ruler/caliper, scale reference, or visible part grouping.
- Reject duplicates unless they show a different face, side, or stack order.
- Final selected set should cover:
  - large main tub cushion
  - small main tub cushion
  - sleeve/crush tube
  - cup/seat washer
  - shim pack
  - front support isolator/pad if present
