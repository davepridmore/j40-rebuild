## Release Checks Before Road Validation

Inspect all five wheels/tyres, including the spare if it will be carried:

1. Record tyre brand, model, size, load/speed rating, and DOT/date code from each tyre.
2. Reject any tyre with sidewall cracking, exposed cord, bulges, deep cuts, bead damage, or repair plugs in unsafe locations.
3. Check tread depth across inner, centre, and outer ribs; note uneven wear, cupping, feathering, or shoulder damage.
4. Remove enough dirt to inspect both sidewalls and the bead line.
5. Check wheel rims for bead-seat rust, bends, cracks, oval lug holes, damaged valve holes, and missing/loose wheel weights.
6. Replace valve stems unless already known recent and pliable.
7. Pressure-test for overnight loss.
8. Balance every road wheel after cleaning and before alignment/road test.
9. Confirm lug nuts match wheel seat type and thread engagement, then torque to the vehicle torque sheet.
10. After Ironman suspension installation, check tyre clearance lock-to-lock and through axle articulation before road use.
