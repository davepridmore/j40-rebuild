Please quote the lines below by ID. For normal rubber hose stock, quote by material, ID, rating, and stated length. Required general confirmations for every quoted line:

- Material compound or product grade.

- Price per line and lead time.

## Measurements And Photos To Send With Samples

For the rubber hose quote, send these with the old hoses where available:

- Old hose laid flat beside a tape measure.
- End ID, end OD, wall thickness, and free length in mm.
- Any visible printed hose markings.
- Photos of both fitted spigots/necks/barbs with caliper or ruler.
- Required bend/offset direction marked with tape or pen.
- Clamp land length and available straight section.

For molded radiator hoses:

- Radiator upper/lower neck OD.
- Thermostat/water-pump neck OD.
- Fan, belt, shroud, crossmember, and radiator support clearance photos.
- Old hose free length only as a check, not as a straight-hose order.

For the formed coolant pipe and connector hoses:

- Send the physical pipe sample if possible.
- Pipe OD, wall thickness, bead height, bead position, and straight clamp land each end.
- Overall centerline length, bend radii, bend clocking, and offset.
- Connector hose ID/OD, free length, overlap length, and kink risk in the fitted position.

For fuel and vacuum hose:

- Barb OD for every hose end.
- Final route photo with clips/support points.
- Heat, exhaust, belt, and chassis chafe clearance.
- Fuel hose printed rating close-up.

For brake/clutch hydraulic assemblies:

- Old complete assembly, not just hose length.
- Free length in mm.
- End fitting style, thread, banjo/seat type, bracket groove, and retaining clip style.
- Full droop/steering movement clearance photos after suspension is installed.

For hard lines:

- Old line as a bend template where possible.
- Tube OD.
- Flare type, fitting thread, union/tee style, and seat type. Current brake-line working basis is Toyota-style `double / inverted` flare from May 14 installed-fitting and flare side-view photos, but confirm from a straight-on removed line end and opened seat before fabrication.
- Clip spacing and pass-through/grommet points.

## Hard Rules

- No used hoses, used hydraulic assemblies, used hard lines, used clamps, or used clips for final installation.
- No clear PVC, washer hose, soft water pipe, or unmarked rubber pipe for fuel.
- No generic rubber hose for brake or clutch hydraulics.
- No bare copper for brake, clutch, or fuel hard lines.
- No fuel hose as a substitute for coolant/heater hose.
- No coolant-only EPDM for oil breather or oil-mist service.
- Do not coat the formed coolant pipe until it passes dry-fit and pressure test.
