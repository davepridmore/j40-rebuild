## Recommendation

Preferred direction after the Vitz photo review: stop chasing donor dashboard HVAC cases and source a universal under-dash evaporator/blower assembly by measured model family. Ask first for `BEU-202`, `BEU-404`, or `BEU-848` style units, because these have regular rectangular cases and are easier to hide or build a plenum around than a molded OEM dashboard box.

Do not buy the pictured OLX Vitz box blind. User photo review on 2026-05-14 shows a full OEM molded HVAC case with a protruding blower scroll, large case volume, and a dash-specific outlet shape. It may work only if dimensions prove it can hide under the J40 dash and accept a sealed outlet plenum. Treat it as measure-first / likely too bulky, not as the default purchase.

## Buy Specification

Ask the supplier/shop for:

- Complete evaporator/blower case, not a bare cooling coil.
- 12V blower motor with at least 3 working speeds and a supplied resistor/control or wiring pigtail.
- R134a-compatible evaporator/TXV/fittings, or clear fitting sizes for new barrier hoses.
- Drain nipple with enough fall to route condensate outside the cabin.
- Case dimensions and photos from all sides before travel or deposit.
- Outlet count and outlet OD, or a flat face that can accept a sealed plenum and 2 to 4 duct takeoffs.
- Return-air path that can be fitted with a serviceable grille/filter.
- Bench blower test plus nitrogen pressure/vacuum leak test before payment.
- A body envelope that can be cardboard-mocked in the J40 before buying; large OEM dash cases are rejected unless the mock-up proves fit.

Reject:

- Bare evaporator coils without a blower/case.
- 24V bus/truck units.
- Visible-only hang-on units that cannot be ducted or hidden cleanly.
- Large full-OEM dash cases with molded outlets and protruding blower scrolls unless measured mock-up proves fit.
- Cracked cases, missing drain, oil staining, corroded core, seized blower, noisy bearings, or missing harness pigtails.
- Any final A/C hose crimping before the evaporator, condenser, compressor, receiver-drier, and firewall bulkhead positions are fixed.

## Call Text

Need a compact 12V evaporator/blower for a hidden A/C install in an old Land Cruiser. Please quote the smallest universal under-dash unit you have, preferably BEU-202, low-profile BEU-404, or short BEU-848 style. Large Vitz-style full dash boxes are probably too bulky. Please send dimensions with tape measure, all-side photos, outlet photos, fitting photos, drain photo, voltage/current, and price. Unit must include blower, evaporator, TXV/expansion valve, drain, thermostat/control, and wiring pigtail; it must be blower-tested and leak-tested before purchase. Visible hang-on is acceptable only if it can be hidden and ducted from behind a slim outlet panel.
