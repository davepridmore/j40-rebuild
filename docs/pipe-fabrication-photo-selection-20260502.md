## Selection Rules

- Keep photos that show the full pipe route, bend clocking, hose overlap, clamp land, end bead/flare, bracket, branch, or mounting relationship.
- Prefer images with a tape, caliper, ruler, or visible vehicle/sample orientation.
- Reject duplicates unless they show a different face, bend, end, or scale reference.
- Do not approve production from photos alone where OD, wall, bead, or bend clocking controls fit.
