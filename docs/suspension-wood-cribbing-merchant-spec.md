# Suspension Wood Cribbing Scout / Fabricator Spec

Give this to the timber merchant, scout, or workshop for the suspension support blocks.

Control package: [suspension_wood_cribbing_rev_a](../data/manual/fabrication/suspension_wood_cribbing_rev_a/README.md)

Primary drawing: [j40_suspension_wood_cribbing_rev_a_dimension_sheet.pdf](../data/manual/fabrication/suspension_wood_cribbing_rev_a/j40_suspension_wood_cribbing_rev_a_dimension_sheet.pdf)

## Copy To Merchant

I need **8 dry hardwood blocks** at **300 x 150 x 75 mm**, plus **4 dry hardwood wedge chocks** at **200 x 100 mm**, **75 mm rear height**, **25 mm blunt nose**. Use sheesham/shisham, kikar/acacia, oak, ash, or similar dense hardwood. No plywood, MDF, chipboard, wet wood, softwood, cracked pieces, oil, or paint.

## Order

Supply **12 total finished pieces** of dry, solid hardwood:

| `SWC-CHOCK-001` | 4 | Dry hardwood wedge, `200 x 100 mm`, `75 mm` rear, `25 mm` nose | <img src="../data/manual/fabrication/suspension_wood_cribbing_rev_a/swc_wedge_chock_rev_a.svg" width="120" alt="SWC-CHOCK-001 wedge chock"> |

If the merchant cannot cut the wedge chocks cleanly, buy **4 extra 200 x 100 x 75 mm solid hardwood blanks** and let the workshop cut the taper from the drawing.

## Quick Check

- Pieces sit flat without rocking.
- Wedges have a blunt `25 mm` nose, not a thin feather edge.
- Wood is dry, solid, raw/unfinished hardwood.
- Reject wet/soft wood, board material, cracks, splits, paint/oil, and large knots on bearing faces.

## Use Note

These blocks are for cribbing and stabilising during the Ironman suspension/brake work. They supplement rated jack stands, a rated trolley jack, and axle support; they are not a substitute for rated stands.
