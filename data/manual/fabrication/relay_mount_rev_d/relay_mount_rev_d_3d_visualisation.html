Assembly Read

Package role
Simplified relay-box support: flat base and insulating sheet under the relay's uncovered bottom face.
Load path
The flat aluminium base bolts to the battery-stand ladder through exposed slots; the insulating sheet sits directly under the relay box's large uncovered bottom face.
Service intent
Keep the covered/removable face accessible above the base, transfer bottom-face relay fixing holes from the actual box after orientation is confirmed, and use the base slots for stand attachment.
